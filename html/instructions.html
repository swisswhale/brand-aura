HOW TO PLAY BRANDAURA

Home Play the Game
  1. Click Play the Game.
  2. You will be presented with a random automotive logo.
  3. Type your answer in the box below the logo.
  4. Hit Submit.
  5. You will receive feedback on your answer and earn 1 point for every correct answer.
  6. See how many you can get right out of 10!

Good luck and have fun!
